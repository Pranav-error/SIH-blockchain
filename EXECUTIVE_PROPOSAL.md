## 💡 HOW IT WORKS (Simple 4-Step Process)

### Step 1: Mobile Collection App
**Collector uses smartphone at harvest location**
- GPS automatically captured (accuracy: ±10 meters)
- App validates: "Are you in authorized Ashwagandha zone?"
- Environmental data recorded (temperature, humidity, season)
- Photos uploaded with geo-tags
- **Blockchain record created** with cryptographic fingerprint

### Step 2: Processing & Quality Testing
**Processor scans batch QR code**
- Links processing data to collection record
- Laboratory adds test results (active compounds, contaminants)
- Quality grade auto-calculated (A+ to F scoring)
- **All steps cryptographically linked** via Merkle tree

### Step 3: Manufacturing Integration
**Your production team accesses verified batches**
- Dashboard shows: Origin, quality grade, collector reputation
- Auto-generates compliance certificates
- Links raw materials to final product batches
- **Complete batch genealogy** maintained

### Step 4: Consumer Verification
**Customer scans QR code on product**
- See origin map (state/district level - privacy preserved)
- View harvest date, quality grade, certifications
- Complete journey timeline visualization
- **Instant trust building** without revealing business secrets
