## THE PROBLEM

**30-40% of Ayurvedic products** contain adulterated herbs. Indian herbal exports face **‚Çπ2,000+ Cr annual rejections** due to lack of source verification. Consumers cannot verify if "Himalayan" herbs actually came from the Himalayas.

---

## OUR SOLUTION

**HerBlock** is a blockchain-based traceability system that validates the **GPS location of herb collection** at the blockchain level before any data is recorded. If a collector tries to claim herbs from an unauthorized location, the **blockchain itself rejects the transaction**.
