## 2. THE PROBLEM WE ARE SOLVING

The Indian herbal and Ayurvedic industry faces a verified and growing crisis:

- **30–40% of commercially traded herbs** are adulterated, substituted, or misrepresented in origin
- **₹2,000+ Crore in annual export rejections** due to inability to prove source authenticity
- **EU and US regulators** are tightening requirements — GACP (Good Agricultural and Collection Practices) certification now demands GPS-verified origin records
- **Consumer trust is eroding** — premium brands like Himalaya are increasingly vulnerable to reputational risk from contaminated supply chains they did not cause

The core issue is not dishonesty at the manufacturing level — it is the **absence of a verifiable, tamper-proof record** from the point of collection to the point of production. Paper-based systems and centralized databases can be forged. The problem requires a fundamentally different architecture.

---

## 3. OUR SOLUTION — HERBLOCK

**HerBlock** is a blockchain-based traceability platform purpose-built for the herbal and Ayurvedic supply chain.

### How It Works (Plain Language)

1. A herb collector in the field opens the HerBlock mobile app and initiates a collection record
2. The app captures GPS coordinates, timestamp, environmental conditions (temperature, humidity), a geo-tagged photograph, and the collector's identity
3. This data is submitted to the blockchain **where the smart contract itself validates** whether the GPS coordinates fall within an authorized collection zone
4. If the location is unauthorized, the blockchain **mathematically rejects the transaction** — it cannot be bypassed by any app-level manipulation
5. If valid, an immutable, cryptographically signed record is created — permanently traceable from collector to consumer
6. At every stage downstream (processing, testing, manufacturing, distribution), a new record is added — each linked to the previous, forming an unbreakable chain
7. The final product carries a QR code — any consumer or regulator can scan it and see the **complete, verified journey** of that herb

### What Makes This Different

| Existing Approaches | HerBlock |
|---------------------|----------|
| Paper-based records (easily forged) | Blockchain-immutable, cannot be altered |
| App validates GPS, then sends to database | **Blockchain validates GPS inside consensus** |
| Centralized database (single point of failure) | Distributed ledger (no single authority) |
| Trust the company's word | **Trust the mathematics** |
| Post-incident verification | **Pre-consensus rejection of fraud** |

The key technical differentiator — validated by REVA University R&D — is that geographic origin validation happens **at the consensus layer**, not at the application layer. This is a patented innovation (patent application filed with the Indian Patent Office, currently under examination).

### What We Are Proposing

A **90-day structured pilot** — at no financial cost to Himalaya — to demonstrate HerBlock's capabilities on a real Himalaya supply chain segment.

### Pilot Scope

| Parameter | Detail |

| **Cost to Himalaya** | Zero — pilot is fully subsidized |

- Access to 1 collection zone (introduction to 10–20 existing collectors)
- Coordination with 1 internal point of contact (R&D / supply chain team)

### What HerBlock Delivers

- Full deployment of the traceability system for the pilot scope
- Mobile app for collectors (iOS & Android)
- Dashboard for Himalaya's internal team
- QR code generation for final product
- Complete audit trail and pilot report at 90 days
