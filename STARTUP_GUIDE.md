## ğŸ“ COLLEGE IPR CELL DISCUSSION POINTS

### What to Discuss

1. **IP Ownership Agreement**
   - Who owns the patent? (You vs College vs Joint)
   - Revenue sharing if college claims partial ownership
   - Typical: Student keeps IP, college gets acknowledgment + small royalty

2. **Incubation Support**
   - Does college have an incubator?
   - Can they provide seed funding?
   - Office space / infrastructure?

3. **Mentorship**
   - Faculty advisor for technical guidance
   - Industry connections
   - Patent attorney referrals

4. **Filing Support**
   - Does college cover patent filing costs?
   - Legal assistance for drafting
   - Prior art search help

### Sample Agreement Terms

| Term | Favorable for You | Typical College Ask |
|------|-------------------|---------------------|
| IP Ownership | 100% yours | Joint ownership |
| Royalty to College | 0-2% of revenue | 5-10% of revenue |
| Acknowledgment | In publications | Logo usage rights |
| First Refusal | None | Investment rights |

**Negotiation Tip**: If you developed this independently (not as part of coursework/thesis), you have stronger claim to 100% ownership.
